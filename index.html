Our mission:

With the rise of hustle culture and working overtime, working individuals aged 18-64 are losing touch with their bodies and their health. We want to make sure that these individuals are maintaining not only their physical health, but their mental wellbeings as well. Over just 4 weeks of habit-tracking, users are able to revisit their old habits in their profile history and access analytics of their lifestyle growth!
